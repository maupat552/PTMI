Ministry Focal Programs

  • 1. One Night with Grace; overnight service – Takes place once in every two months.
    • 2. Grace crusades; Teaching crusades to bring the lost to Christ and win souls to him. The emphasis is teaching the uncompromising word of his grace. 3. Evangel Reach; this is a mass evangelism program. We reach out with outstretched arms to all. The purpose is to bring them to the master through meeting their needs; spirit, soul, and body. Different locations are flooded with light. It is slated for once every three months. 4. Chambers of the word; this is a panel discussion that allows all in to be directly involved in the teaching of the word and thereby augmenting in their dissemination formats. This happens every once in a month. 5. Present Truth Conference; this is a main conference of the ministry it handles unlike others cross cutting issues making it desirable. Its location is not static. It happens twice a year. 6. Finance convention; it deals with aspects of finance as a whole. It takes place once every year. 7. Shachah; worship and praise convention; the revealing of verities that are tied to his essence are made possible here. This takes place once every year. 8. Main Location Fellowship; the gathering of all location fellowships and having communion. This happens once every two months. 9. Paraklets Conference, this where comfort is corporately Ministered. It takes place once in a year. 10. Mimshach Conference; Service is ministered here to promote the gospel. It happens once every year. 11. Marriage conference; the ideas of both marriage to God and Man is emphasized here. This takes place once every year.29 12. Leadership conference; aspects and attributes of leadership are communicated here. It takes place once every year. 13. Excellence Summit; the idea of throwing beyond others in all spheres is emphasized here. This always takes place at the end of every year. 14. Missions; this is a very strategic program of any field. A time frame cannot be tagged on it because it can happen any time. This is good. 15. Main Campus Fellowships; the campusers are accorded the opportunity to receive this word with eloquence. It’s here that they exercise their mandate. It takes place regularly as there are many campuses. 16. Miracle Fruits’ services the opportunity to manifest all that God has said and given is important. In this program less of talking is done and more of doing is emphasized. Miracle fruits of healing, raising the dead, finance, etc will be tangibly exhibited. It takes place once every four months. 17. Thanks Giving Service; there are two that take place. One is the intro thanks giving service which takes place in January of every year. Its main purpose is to thank the Lord for our entry into the year. The other is the outro thanks giving service which takes place in December every year. Its purpose is to thank the Lord for the completion of the outgoing year. 18. Doxa Saturday: takes place every last Saturday of the month. A teaching ministry that talks place every last Saturday of the month. 19. Emeth; the Gospel Music Extravaganza: this is a gospel music ministry every 25th December. 20. Isaac Sunday: this a Christian standup comedy stance that takes place every second Sunday of the month in churches. 21. Vade Mecum Reading Club and Bible Study: the book reading club of PTMI and Bible Study. 22. Back to Bible Meet: Taking the church back to the bible. It is to take place once every Two months. 23. Ask the Teacher: this the opportunity to ask the teacher biblical questions and Experiences. 24. Youth Conference: this is a gathering of those between the ages of 10 – 35 years. They are to be guided in the way of the Lord. This will take place once a year. 25. Huios Conference: this is a meeting of all the children in the ministry. It covers all below the age of 18 years. It will take place once in two years. 26. Mayim Submersion: this is what is commonly called water baptism; the outside symbol of an inside occurrence. It will take place twice every year.
